MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025 ("Effective Date") by and between ServiceProvider Inc. ("Provider") and ClientCorp LLC. ("Client").

1. Services Provided
Provider agrees to deliver consulting and development services as outlined in the Statement of Work attached hereto. Provider warrants that all services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.

2. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to treat all exchanged proprietary data and trade secrets as strictly confidential. Neither party shall disclose such data to any unauthorized outside entities without express written approval.

3. Termination for Convenience
Client may terminate this Agreement at any time for any reason by providing Provider with at least sixty (60) days advance written notice.

4. Uncapped Liability
NOTWITHSTANDING ANYTHING TO THE CONTRARY IN THIS AGREEMENT, THE PROVIDER'S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY ARISING FROM GROSS NEGLIGENCE, WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, OR BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS SHALL BE ABSOLUTELY UNCAPPED AND UNLIMITED.

5. Exclusivity
Provider agrees that during the term of this Agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months thereafter, Provider shall not offer similar platform development services to any direct competitor of the Client.

6. Non-Solicit of Employees
During the term of this Agreement and for two (2) years after its termination, Provider shall not directly or indirectly encourage any employee of the Client to leave their employment.
